How the leaderboard works and answers to common questions.

Point system & rankings

The leaderboard tracks performance and Total Points. Rankings can be based on points or on performance stats:

  • Total Points — Combined score from wins, performance, and events (used for overall rank).
  • W — Tournament wins (matches won).
  • L — Losses (matches lost).
  • KDR — Kill/Death ratio (kills ÷ deaths). Higher is better.
  • TKOs — Technical knockouts (when a player spec kills the other player).

Use the leaderboard filters (All, Main, Zerk, Pure) to see rankings by bracket. Click column headers to sort by Points, W, L, KDR, or TKOs.

Brackets: Main, Zerk, Pure

Fighters compete in build-based brackets. Main — main accounts; Zerk — zerker builds; Pure — pure builds. Each bracket has its own leaderboard view.

Viewer FAQ

When are events?
Check the Event info at the top and the Upcoming Fights section. Times are listed per event.
Where do I watch?
See the Streams & Platforms section and the event stream links in the header. Tune in on the listed platforms.
How do I apply to fight?
Use the Fighter Application link below. Submit your in-game name, build, experience, and contact details.
How is a match won?
Matches are decided by round wins or by the rules set for that event (e.g. best-of rounds). The stream and event info will state the format.
How can I volunteer or sponsor?
Use the Volunteer application for staff roles. For sponsorship, reach out via the Discord or contact listed on the site.
